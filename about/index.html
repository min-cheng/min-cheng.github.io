About

I attend Swarthmore College and will graduate in May 2018.
I study Sociology, Anthropology, Education, and Economics.
On breaks, I return to my home of Baltimore County, Maryland.
I plan to pursue a PhD and become a college professor at a liberal arts college.
I sing in and arrange for Mixed Company, Swarthmore's oldest co-ed a cappella group.


I'm currently learning Python.
I'm deeply interested in anthropologically researching social media, the education of
Scottish folk music, and the singing of spiritual songs by predominantly non-black choirs.
I've never found my one true passion because I haven't been able to decide between all of them.
